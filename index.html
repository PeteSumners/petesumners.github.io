We’re not here to game the system. We’re here to plant, to tend, to learn to live again— in harmony with the Creator. Not as wolves among wolves, but as sheep who know the voice of their Shepherd.

This isn’t about becoming kings. It's about ceasing to be slaves. A peasant with land, family, and peace is freer than a prince who fears tomorrow.

Not because it's clever. But because it's right. Everything good was made through Christ—and we aim to live like we believe that.
